We are excited to invite you to Edinburgh, Scotland's capital city, for the fourth edition of the Young Researchers' Symposium on Plant Photobiology (YRSPP).

YRSPP is designed for (and organised by) early-career researchers (ECRs) in the field of plant photobiology, and YRSPP 2026 will be a gathering of young researchers investigating the ways plants perceive, transduce and integrate light and circadian signals in their development and growth.

The primary goal of YRSPP is to showcase the cutting-edge work of young plant photobiologists, offering networking opportunities to promote international cross-disciplinary collaboration, knowledge exchange and innovation in plant photobiology. This comes at a crucial juncture in the advancement of plant science research, as numerous global challenges highlight the need to harness the potential of plants.
